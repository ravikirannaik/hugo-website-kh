This paper examines the relationship between innovation and internationalisation strategies of emerging-market firms. It investigates how firms in developing economies navigate the dual challenges of innovating while expanding internationally, analyzing the conditions under which these strategies complement or conflict with each other.

Under review at *American Business Review*. Submitted November 2024.
