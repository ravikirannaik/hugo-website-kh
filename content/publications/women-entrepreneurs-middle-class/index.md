This book provides a comprehensive analysis of women entrepreneurs in the new Indian middle class, combining macro-level quantitative survey data with micro-level qualitative insights. It examines the challenges, opportunities, and strategies of women entrepreneurs across different sectors, contributing to our understanding of gender dynamics in Indian entrepreneurship and the evolving middle class.
