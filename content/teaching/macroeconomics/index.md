---
title: "Macroeconomics"
date: "2023-01-01"
---

Undergraduate course introducing core macroeconomic concepts and models. Covers national income accounting, long-run economic growth (Solow model), business cycle analysis, and the tools of fiscal and monetary policy, with applications to the Indian and global economy.

Key topics: GDP measurement, savings and investment, productivity, IS-LM model, aggregate demand and supply, unemployment and inflation, Phillips Curve, fiscal policy, monetary policy, open economy macroeconomics, macroeconomic policy in developing economies.
