---
title: "Public Economics"
date: "2023-06-01"
---

Elective course examining the role of government in the economy. Covers the theory and practice of taxation, public goods provision, externalities, and social welfare programs, emphasizing applications to Indian public policy.

Key topics: market failures and government intervention, public goods and collective action, externalities and environmental policy, taxation theory and practice, social insurance and welfare programs, public expenditure analysis, fiscal federalism in India.
