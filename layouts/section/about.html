I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at FLAME University, Pune, India. My research focuses on applied labour economics, platform/gig work, and intergenerational inequality. I investigate how platform workers navigate precarity in global digital labor markets, with a focus on skill specialization, wage determination, and informal employment in the Global South.

Most of my publications and working papers are available on this site, along with information about courses I teach, various research projects, data, and related resources, and some occasional writing. My Curriculum Vitae has more detail, if you need it.
